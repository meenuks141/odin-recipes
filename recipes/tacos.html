Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with a folded tortilla filled with seasoned meat, vegetables, and toppings.

They are quick to prepare, flavorful, and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce (shredded)
  • Tomatoes (diced)
  • Cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook ground beef with chopped onion and garlic.
  3. Add taco seasoning and stir well.
  4. Warm the taco shells in the oven.
  5. Fill each shell with the cooked meat.
  6. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
