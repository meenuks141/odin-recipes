Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the beef with garlic and onion.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Let cool and serve.
